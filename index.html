MY TRADING WEB

MTW is a price player that works with an free API from Alpha Vantage where you can see how the prices are going through specific periods of time until the most currently updated price in only one candle.

How Data Player works

MTW also allows you to calculate how many lots you have to risk on each trade, you only have to fill the three input fields that are your total capital, the cost of your stop loss with 0.01 lots and the percentage that you want to risk.
